News Spot

News Spot is a better way to read and share news. Users can search from hundreds of recent articles, provided by NewsAPI, and share them or save them for later in "collections." Users can see recent activity from others as well. I was Design Lead on the project, which took 3 weeks in a small team of developers.

NewsAPI, React, Redux, Nodejs, Mongo, Express
